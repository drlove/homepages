About IOSN ASEAN+3

The International Open Source Network ASEAN+3 is a collaborative platform that supports the adoption, development, and governance of open source technologies in the ASEAN region and partner economies.

IOSN ASEAN+3 brings together governments, academia, civil society, and the private sector to strengthen digital capacity through open standards and open source solutions.

Mission

  • Promote open source as a strategic public good
  • Strengthen regional digital cooperation
  • Build local technical and governance capacity
  • Encourage sustainable and inclusive innovation

Focus Areas

  • Open source policy and governance
  • Digital public infrastructure
  • Interoperability and open standards
  • Capacity building and training
  • Community-driven innovation

Key Activities

  • Regional workshops and training programs
  • Technical labs and pilot implementations
  • Policy dialogues and knowledge exchanges
  • Support for open source communities
  • Documentation and best-practice sharing
