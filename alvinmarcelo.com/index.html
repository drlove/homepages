Current positions

Dr. Alvin B. Marcelo is a general and trauma surgeon by training and a recognized leader in medical informatics and digital health.

    President of the Asia-Pacific Association of Medical Informatics (APAMI)
    Director-Treasurer of the Asia eHealth Information Network (AeHIN)
    President of the Philippine Society for Digital Health
    Director-Corporate Secretary at the Asia OpenRAN Academy
    Professorial lecturer at the Medical Informatics Unit and Department of Surgery of the University of the Philippines College of Medicine
    Founder and convener, Standards and Interoperability Lab (SILab) at UP Manila

Work experience

    Dr. Marcelo has advised international organizations including the World Health Organization, Asian Development Bank, UNICEF, and USAID on eHealth and health information systems.

Education

    He completed a postdoctoral fellowship in medical informatics at the U.S. National Library of Medicine in Bethesda, Maryland, with research interests in telepathology, mobile computing, and bibliometric analysis of MEDLINE content. He is one of the pioneering inventors of the RxBox and holds certifications in CGEIT, TOGAF, ArchiMate, and COBIT. He is a Fellow of the Philippine College of Surgeons.
